PointScheduler

Manage Therapists, Sessions & Patients Effortlessly

Focus more on care — let PointScheduler handle the management.

PointScheduler is a complete management system created for therapy centers, therapists, and patients. Designed especially for Autism-focused centers but suitable for physiotherapy, speech therapy, OT, mental health clinics, rehabilitation centers, and more.

Key Features

Center Management

Manage centers, admins, rooms, slots & therapy types.

Therapist Management

Add therapists, availability, assignments & attendance.

Patient & Autism Care

Track ABA goals, progress, reports & parent feedback.

Smart Scheduling

Auto-slot system with drag & drop calendar.
